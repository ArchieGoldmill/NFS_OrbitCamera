Need for Speed - Orbit car camera 1.0

Supported games:
- Most Wanted
- Carbon

Description:
This mod allows to rotate camera around the car while driving around.

How to install:
1. Drop everything from appropriate mod folder to your scripts folder.
2. Change settings in "scripts/NFSMWOrbitCamera.ini" or "scripts/NFSCOrbitCamera.ini".

Distribution:
You are NOT allowed to distribute or reupload this mod.
